About Smart Sphere Classroom

Smart Sphere Classroom is an innovative online learning platform designed to provide an interactive and engaging digital classroom experience. Inspired by Google Classroom, our platform integrates **advanced features** to enhance productivity and efficiency for both students and educators.

Key Features:

  • 📌 **Custom Scheduling & Notifications** – Automated reminders for task deadlines.
  • 📚 **Assignment Management** – Faculty can post assignments, and students can submit their work online.
  • 📝 **Test & Quiz Platform** – Seamless test assignments with **automatic evaluation**.
  • 🤖 **AI-powered Chatbot** – A built-in assistant to answer student queries in real-time.
  • 📆 **Calendar & To-Do List** – Organize schedules and keep track of deadlines.
  • 🎮 **Interactive Quiz Games** – Enhance learning through engaging quiz-based challenges.
  • 🔒 **Secure Login & Dashboard** – A user-friendly interface for easy access.

Our Tech Stack:

We have used **modern technologies** to ensure a smooth and efficient user experience:

  • 🚀 **Backend:** Node.js (Express.js) for fast response times.
  • 💾 **Database:** MongoDB for secure and scalable data storage.
  • 📡 **Real-time Updates:** Firebase for instant notifications and event scheduling.
  • 🧠 **AI Integration:** Gen Flash AI model for accurate chatbot responses.
