About Smart Sphere Classroom

Revolutionizing education with smart scheduling, real-time feedback, and AI-powered support.

Smart Sphere Classroom is an advanced online educational platform developed by a team of engineering students to overcome the limitations of traditional learning systems. It integrates features such as automatic test evaluation, intelligent assignment reminders, AI-powered chatbot for academic queries, and interactive learning modules. The system supports both students and educators through real-time notifications and seamless classroom management, improving productivity and engagement.

Our Mission

Our platform addresses key challenges found in existing LMS systems, such as delayed feedback, lack of timely reminders, and poor student engagement. By combining the power of automation and artificial intelligence, we provide a structured learning environment that keeps both students and faculty connected.

Key Features

AI-Powered Chatbot

Built using the Gen-Flash model, it helps students get instant solutions to academic queries, reducing dependency on faculty and fostering self-learning.

Automated Test Management

Faculty members can assign, evaluate, and monitor test results with minimal effort.

Real-Time Notifications

Integration with Firebase enables push/email notifications for test reminders and assignment deadlines, ensuring students never miss an important task.

Interactive Learning

Gamified modules like quiz games and coding puzzles make the learning experience more engaging and enjoyable.

Technology Stack

Smart Sphere Classroom is built using modern tools and frameworks, including React.js, Node.js, MongoDB, and Firebase, ensuring scalability, speed, and flexibility. With a robust backend and structured frontend, we deliver a seamless user experience across all devices.
